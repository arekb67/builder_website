Our story
Started local,
stayed personal

BlockBox Ltd started with a handful of projects in south London and a commitment to doing things properly. No cutting corners, no disappearing mid-build, no vague quotes that balloon once the work begins.

Over the years we've grown — but we've kept the things that made those early projects work: a dedicated project manager for every job, honest pricing, and the kind of communication that means you always know what's happening in your own home.

Today we work across all of Greater London, delivering everything from kitchen renovations to full new builds. The scale has changed. The standards haven't.

What we stand for
Our values

Transparency

Clear, detailed quotes with no hidden costs. We'll tell you what things cost, why, and what to expect — before any work begins.

Craftsmanship

We take pride in the quality of our work. Every finish, every joint, every detail is held to the same high standard — because your home deserves it.

Communication

Weekly updates, a dedicated project manager, and an open-door policy. You'll never have to chase us for information about your own project.

Respect

We respect your home, your time, and your budget. We leave the site tidy at the end of every day and stick to agreed timelines.

Accountability

If something isn't right, we fix it — promptly and at our cost. We stand behind every project with a workmanship warranty.

Enjoyment

Renovating your home should be exciting. We've built our process to take the stress out and put the enjoyment in.
