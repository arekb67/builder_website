Common questions
Frequently asked questions

Where in London do you work?

We cover all of Greater London, including central, south, east, west, and north London boroughs.

How much does a typical renovation cost?

Projects range from around £15,000 for a bathroom renovation to £500,000+ for a full new build. We'll give you a detailed estimate within a few days of our initial consultation.

Do I need planning permission?

It depends on the scope. Many projects fall under permitted development, but we'll advise you during the consultation and handle applications if needed.

How long will my project take?

A kitchen typically takes 4–8 weeks; a loft conversion 8–12 weeks; a full renovation 12–24 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline in our quote.

Can I stay in my home during the work?

In most cases, yes. We plan the work in phases to minimise disruption. For major structural work, we'll discuss options with you upfront.

What happens if something goes wrong?

We're fully insured and stand behind our work. Any issues are addressed promptly at our cost, and we offer a workmanship warranty on all projects.
